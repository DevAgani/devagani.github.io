**Collaboration** · iOS · Music · Multi-language

A Seventh Day Adventist hymnal app containing all 300 songs from the *Christ In Song* hymnal, translated into multiple African languages including English, Shona, Ndebele, IsiXhosa, Tswana, Sotho, Chichewa, Venda, Swahili, Tonga, and Xitsonga. Users can switch between hymnals and languages for an inclusive worship experience.
